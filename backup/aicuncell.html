Overview

Ai Cun Cell is an end-to-end cord blood management platform designed to break geographical barriers. By addressing significant information asymmetry and the "black box" nature of the storage process, the platform provides a comprehensive digital solution — from medical registration and collection to long-term monitoring — instilling a sense of security for new parents across a 20-year emotional and financial commitment.

Problem

Three layers of friction preventing parents from trusting cord blood banking.

01
Information Transparency

Traditional cord blood storage feels like a "black box" — severe lack of real-time updates leaves users in the dark regarding sample safety and status.

02
Process Complexity

The legacy application process involves heavy paperwork and physical constraints. Navigating medical appointments across regions creates significant friction for expectant parents.

03
Long-term Trust Gap

Cord blood banking is a 20-year commitment. Building a high-integrity digital platform was essential to instil reliability from the very first interaction.

Process

Discover → Define → Iterate — two rounds of usability testing to earn parental trust.

01 Discover

Market analysis and in-depth interviews with 20+ expectant parents. Identified "complex contracts" and "geographical barriers" as primary pain points.

02 Define

Developed user personas and mapped task flows — specifically optimising the high-friction experience of multi-page medical form completion.

03 Iteration

Two rounds of usability testing. After identifying user hesitation during payment in round one, implemented progress bars and security certification badges to enhance trust and clarity.

5K+ Users in week one
30% CSAT increase

Industry's first full-lifecycle cord blood application. Users praised the convenience of the new "Online Contract Signing" feature.
