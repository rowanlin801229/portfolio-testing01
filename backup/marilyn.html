Overview

A dedicated anniversary campaign website for Marilyn, Taiwan's leading luxury brand for bespoke medical-grade corsets. I successfully translated the brand's "Elegant & Professional" visual DNA into a high-efficiency digital experience — optimising information architecture and implementing a centrosymmetric design aesthetic to strengthen trust among potential customers during the brand's most critical sales period.

Challenge

Translating "Elegant & Dependable" into a frictionless digital campaign that converts.

Complexity

The anniversary event — the brand's most critical sales period — required a sophisticated platform that could showcase bespoke craftsmanship while handling multiple product categories, complex promotional tiers, and a seamless booking process. The challenge: making premium complexity feel effortless.

Design Decisions

Four principles anchored in "Refined Temperament."

01 Strategic Branding

Developed a design strategy utilising a sophisticated palette of metallic gold (#B28850) and silver to reflect premium status. Designed custom iconography to communicate body-shaping functions intuitively.

02 Structural Optimisation

Implemented a four-column grid system for special discount sections to enhance readability and allow users to compare complex promotional packages at a glance.

03 Aesthetic Balance

Employed a centrosymmetric design principle on product pages to establish visual equilibrium — fostering security and reassurance for users interacting with high-ticket health products.

04 Frictionless Conversion

Redesigned "Booking" forms with extreme simplicity to prevent cognitive overload, ensuring seamless transition from product discovery to offline appointment scheduling.

Outcome
Validated Excellence

Usability testing with 10 participants praised the platform's clear hierarchy and ease of use.

Business Impact

Post-launch feedback highlighted a significant increase in product sales and successful capture of new potential customers through redesigned lead generation funnels.

Client Affirmation

The client specifically lauded the "reliable and elegant" design, confirming the new interface effectively elevated brand perception during the high-stakes campaign.
